About me

I am Muntasir Mohammad, Specialist, Data Science currently working at Robi Axiata Ltd. I graduated from North South University on 2020 on Computer Science and Engineering (major Machine Learning), securing the honour of Magna Cum Lauda with C.G.P.A - 3.75.

Before graduating, I did an internship at Gaze Technology, which then was an AI firm but now has pivoted to being a Software Firm. During that internship, I worked on Face Spoof Detection as one of their upcoming projects. After graduating, I started to work at NybSys from October 2020 and continued till July 2021. At NybSys, I worked with creating Machine Learning architectures for their upcoming projects, as well as working on their existing products. I had worked on NybFace, which is a Face Recognition based Access Control System. I had worked on developing the backend of NybFace for integrating with web and mobile based applications. I had also worked on creating a production build of Survello, which is a surveillance system. I had also contributed in performing OPS on the products, which included load testing the server, API testing, server hosting etc. We also worked on Big Data using AWS S3, AWS EMR, Apache Spark, Hadoop etc.

At Robi, I work on providing analytics on various existing user facing services of Robi and producing solutions to them. The solutions lie between finding data driven solutions to producing machine learning based services.
